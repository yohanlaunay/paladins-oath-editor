|'''Maze'''

Enter and select one of the open '''Paths'''. Each path leads to a battle but the rewards depends on the path selected. You may only bring 1 Follower with you into the Maze.

Paths:
*'''Lair''' - ''Cost:'' 2 Movement. ''Reward:'' 2 Crystals of Any color
*'''Pit''' - ''Cost:'' 4 Movement. ''Reward:'' 1 Spell
*'''Abyss:''' - ''Cost:'' 6 Movement. ''Reward:'' 1 Relic (draw 2 pick 1)

Only unconquered paths can be entered.
