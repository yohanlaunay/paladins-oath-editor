|'''Quick Attack'''
Quick Attacks require '''twice''' '''the amount of Block''' to block than the Attack value.

''Mods value=5''
|-
|[[File:attack mod brutal.png|center|thumb|100x100px|Savage Attack|link=Special:FilePath/Attack_modifier_brutal.png]]
|'''Savage Attack'''
Savage Attacks will deal '''twice the amount of Damage''' if unblocked.

''Mods value=4''
|-
|[[File:attack mod paralyze.png|center|thumb|100x100px|Paralyzing Attack|link=Special:FilePath/Attack_modifier_paralyze.png]]
|'''Paralyzing Attack'''
Paralyzing Attacks targeted towards the '''Player Character''' will cause the Player to lose all of their Cards.
Paralyzing Attacks targeted towards a '''Follower''' will cause the Follower to be '''Destroyed'''.

''Mods value=2''
|-
|[[File:attack mod poison.png|center|thumb|100x100px|Poison Attack|link=Special:FilePath/Attack_modifier_poison.png]]
|'''Poison Attack'''
Poison  Attacks targeted towards the '''Player Character''' will cause the Player to take an additional '''Wound''' in their '''Discard Pile''' for each '''Wound''' the Attack gave them (in '''Hand''').
Poison Attacks targeted towards a '''Follower''' will cause the Follower to take an additional '''Wound''' for each '''Wound''' the Attack gave them.

''Mods value=1''
|-
|[[File:attack mod cumbersome.png|center|thumb|100x100px|Bulky Attack|link=Special:FilePath/Attack_modifier_cumbersome.png]]
|'''Bulky / Cumbersome Attack'''
Enemies with Bulky or Cumbersome Attacks are typically very '''strong''' but really slow and '''Movement''' points can be used to '''reduce the strength''' of Attack.

''Mods value=6''
|-
|[[File:attack mod assassin.png|center|thumb|100x100px|Marked Attack|link=Special:FilePath/Attack_modifier_assassin.png]]
|'''Marked (Assassin) Attack'''
Enemies with '''Marked''' (or '''Assassin''') Attacks are '''always targeting''' the Player Character. '''Followers''' cannot be used to tank the Damage.

''Mods value=3''
