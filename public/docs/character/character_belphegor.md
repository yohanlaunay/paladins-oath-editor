''Mods GUID="character_belphegor"''

===Backstory===
Belphegor was an outcast but living a peaceful and pious life. When abominations from other planes started appearing Belphegor decided it was time to dust off his trusty weapon and restore the peace.

===Playstyle Tips===
Jack of all trades, can scout, heal, defend, attack and debuff enemies.
