Description for the Paladin '''Zaurahel''' in the game Paladin's Oath.

''Mods GUID="character_zaurahel"''

===Backstory===
Zaurahel was living deep below the surface, lost in prayers to the Dark Gods when the Conjunction of the Planes disrupted the peace. Zaurahel took arms to cleanse the surface dwellers that do not belong there.

===Playstyle Tips===
Can trade Wounds for Mana or Cards. Ideal starting character.
