Description for the Paladin '''Murakh''' in the game Paladin's Oath.

''Mods GUID="character_murakh"''

===Backstory===
Murakh lives communing with the surrounding Elemental Gods. The Conjunction of the planes lead to corruption of everything that was and Murakh will not stand for it. The natural order must return.

===Playstyle Tips===
Natural born leader, ideal for recruiting and leveraging Followers.
