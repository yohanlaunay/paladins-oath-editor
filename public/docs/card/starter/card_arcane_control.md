[[File:arcane_control.png|thumb|180px|Arcane Control Starter Ability Card from Paladin's Oath card game]]This card is a more powerful version of '''[[Arcane Source (Starter Ability)|Arcane Source]]'''.

==='''Basic Action'''===
'''Cost:''' ''Free''

You can use '''1 additional''' Ambient Mana. You may also turn ''Black'' Ambient Mana into a Mana of '''Any''' color.

==='''Advanced Action'''===
'''Cost:''' ''White Mana''

Turn '''2''' Ambient Mana into '''2''' Mana of any color (except ''Gold''). The Ambient Mana will be turned to these colors at the end of the Turn.
===Default Action===
'''Cost:''' ''Free''

Move '''1''', Attack '''1''', Block '''1''' or Leadership '''1'''
