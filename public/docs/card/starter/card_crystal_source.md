==='''Basic Action'''===
'''Cost:''' ''Free''

'''Crystallize''' a Mana of any ''basic'' color. You may pay a ''non-wound'' Card to take this card back into your ''Hand'' at '''End of Turn.'''

'''Cost:''' ''Blue Mana''

Gain a '''Crystal''' of any ''basic'' color. You may pay '''any''' Card (including Wounds) to take this card back into your ''Hand'' at '''End of Turn'''.
