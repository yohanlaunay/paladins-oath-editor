===Basic Action===
'''Cost:''' ''White Mana''

Pay '''1 - 5''' movement to '''Teleport''' that many spaces on the map

===Advanced Action===
'''Cost:''' ''White + Black Mana''

'''Cancel''' targeted Enemy's Attack. Pay '''1''' Movement to target a second Enemy, another '''2''' Movement for a third enemy, etc.

===Default Action===
'''Cost:''' ''Free''

Move '''1''', Attack '''1''', Block '''1''' or Leadership '''1'''
